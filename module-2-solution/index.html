Our Menu

Chicken
We have our fabulous Butter Chicken, some Kadhai Chicken, or some Tawa Chicken. If you're feeling adventurous, you can try our Chicken Masala, our Chicken Curry or even our Chicken Kali Mirch. The Handi Chicken has received very good reviews, as has the Matki Chicken and the Belli Ram Chicken. Our Cream Chicken is our top of the line dish, followed only by the Lemon Chicken and the Chicken Qorma. Some customers do ask for the Rara Chicken and once we've had one ask for Dhaniya Adraki Chicken.
Beef
You can't leave without trying our New York Strip, our Ribye, some Veal or some delicious Baby Ribs. We also have amazing burgers! We can set you up with a Classic Burger, a Cheeseburger, a BBQ Beef Burger, a Mexican Burger or a Beef Steak Burger! Our top of the line are the All-American, the Pepper Jack Bacon, the Mushroom & Swiss, and the Bacon Bleu. Our delicacies are desired all over the world and we go to great deals of effort to have customers as happy as they can possibly be, we encourage you to try!
Fish
Please treat yourself with our Seafood Chowder, our Maple Salmon, or our Tuna Burger! We have some delicacies with Trout, Tuna and Talapia. All of these fish plates can come with special Alfredo Sauce, they can be Thai Style Fried, Sambai Fried, Butter Cream, Tiga Rasa Fried, or Sweet and Sour Fried. They can be accompanied by Hong Kong Steam Sauce, Tropical Sauce, Teow Chew Style Sauce, or just some simple but delicious Golf Sauce. Our kitchen staff comes from countries with oceans and are experts at seafood!
